The Last of Us

A masterpiece of storytelling in gaming

Overview

Released in 2013 by Naughty Dog, The Last of Us is an action-adventure survival horror game set in a post-apocalyptic United States. The story follows Joel, a smuggler tasked with escorting a teenage girl named Ellie across the country in hopes of developing a cure for the fungal infection that has devastated humanity.

Main Characters

  • Joel Miller - A hardened survivor with a tragic past
  • Ellie - A brave 14-year-old girl who may hold the key to a cure
  • Tess - Joel's smuggling partner in Boston
  • Tommy - Joel's younger brother and former Firefly

Cast & Creative Team

  • Troy Baker as Joel
  • Ashley Johnson as Ellie
  • Neil Druckmann - Creative Director/Writer
  • Bruce Straley - Game Director
  • Gustavo Santaolalla - Composer

Reviews
